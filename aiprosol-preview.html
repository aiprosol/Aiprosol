21 FAQs

Questions, answered straight

Do I have to book a call to get started?+

No — Aiprosol is self-serve first. Most clients start with a digital product or the ROI Audit, then upgrade to a managed plan when the numbers prove out. Discovery calls are reserved for enterprise-level enquiries only.

How long does onboarding take?+

Starter and Growth plans are live within 14 days. Enterprise white-glove onboarding takes 3–4 weeks depending on the integration footprint. Most digital products are downloadable within 60 seconds of purchase.

What happens if my automation breaks?+

Every managed plan includes monitoring, alerting, and Arora's auto-recovery layer. If something fails, we know within seconds — and most issues are auto-resolved before you'd notice. SLA response is 24h on Starter, 4h on Growth, 1h on Enterprise.

Do you charge in GBP?+

Yes — all pricing is in British Pounds. We serve clients globally and bill in GBP regardless of your location.

Can I cancel a managed plan anytime?+

Yes. There are no minimum commitments on Starter or Growth — you can cancel at the end of any monthly cycle. Enterprise plans have a 6-month minimum because of the architectural investment, but renewals are month-to-month after that.

What if I'm not technical?+

That's exactly who Aiprosol is built for. Arora handles the technical architecture; you stay focused on your business. Every automation is delivered with plain-English documentation and a video walkthrough.

What tools do you integrate with?+

Anything with an API. Most engagements involve Make, Zapier, n8n, HubSpot, Pipedrive, ActiveCampaign, Calendly, Xero, Slack, Notion, and the major LLM providers. If your stack isn't on the list, we'll either integrate it or tell you honestly that it's not feasible.
