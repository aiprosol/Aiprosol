## POST 25 · Tactical · Build vs Buy

When to build a custom workflow vs use an off-the-shelf tool:

**Buy** when:
— Your need is mainstream
— Your volume is below the SaaS pricing tier where it gets dumb
— Your team will use the tool weekly

**Build** when:
— Off-the-shelf forces a workflow you can't actually adopt
— Volume is high and SaaS pricing punishes scale
— The workflow gives you competitive edge

Most teams over-buy SaaS. Half the stack is replaceable with a focused custom flow.
