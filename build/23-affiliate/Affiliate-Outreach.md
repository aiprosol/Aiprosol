## SENDING DISCIPLINE

- 10 sends/day max — spammy past that
- Tue–Thu, 09:30–11:00 in their timezone
- Personalised first sentence (the `Reading {{newsletterName}}` opener) — generics get filtered
- One follow-up at +5 business days, no second
- Track: opens, clicks, replies, applications submitted
- Cull non-responders after 2 contacts → no more emails

## WHAT HAPPENS WHEN THEY APPLY

1. Application lands in `affiliatepartners` CMS with `applicationStatus = "Pending Review"`
2. Within 5 business days: review and reply (approve / reject / request more info)
3. On approval: send unique affiliate link + dashboard credentials + creative kit
4. First commission tracked from the next sale they refer
5. Monthly payout via Stripe (1st of each month, > £50 threshold)
