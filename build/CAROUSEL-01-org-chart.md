## Canva assembly checklist · 30 minutes

1. **New design** → "Instagram Post" → 1080×1350 → Custom dimensions
2. **Add 10 pages** (one per slide)
3. **Set page background** to `#0A0613` for all
4. **Add brand fonts**: Space Grotesk + Inter (Canva → Uploads → Fonts → upload .ttf from Google Fonts)
5. **Build slide 1** (cover) first — it's the template for the eyebrow chip + footer pattern
6. **Copy slide 1** → modify for slides 2 through 10, swapping content per the spec
7. **For slide 3** (org chart): use Canva's "Lines and shapes" → "Rectangle" tool + the "Line" tool with right angles. Set the corner radius to 12px for the cards. Group everything when done.
8. **For slide 9** (guarantee): use the "Frame" tool to create the gradient-bordered card. Canva's gradient stroke needs ≥2 of the same shape stacked with offset fills.
9. **Export** → All pages as PNG → 10 files
10. **Upload to Instagram** → New post → Multi-image → drag all 10 in order

## Common Canva pitfalls

| Pitfall | Fallback |
|---|---|
| Canva doesn't support full Space Grotesk 800 — kerning looks off | Upload the SG font file from Google Fonts; or sub with Canva's "Anton" for displays (close enough but slightly heavier) |
| Gradient text loses sharpness on export | Export at 2× (Canva Pro feature) — or render the gradient text as a flattened image and place it as an element |
| Org chart connectors don't align cleanly | Hold Shift while drawing lines to lock to 0/45/90° angles; use Canva's "Smart Guides" |
| Slide 7 logos look amateurish | The text-logo treatment is intentional — don't try to source real customer logos until you have written permission from each customer (you don't yet) |
| Colors print/screen-different on phone | Export PNG, view on your phone before posting — Instagram applies its own color processing |

## Why this carousel beats the equivalent video

- **Carousels save better than reels.** IG's "save" metric is the second-strongest reach driver after "share." The org-chart slide is highly save-worthy.
- **Carousels work on LinkedIn too.** Same 10 slides, 1080×1350, ship as a LinkedIn "document" post — gets 4-5× the reach of a regular LinkedIn post.
- **No Higgsfield dependency.** Ship today, regardless of CLI auth status.
- **The org chart slide alone is screenshot-bait.** That's the share trigger that gets the carousel into feeds beyond your existing followers.
