# 🎬 Production notes for a solo founder

You don't need a videographer. Here's what works on a phone:

- **Lighting:** record between 9am and 4pm, near a north-facing window. Skip ring lights — natural is better.
- **Audio:** wired earbuds with the inline mic, not your phone's built-in mic. Bad audio kills retention faster than bad video.
- **Background:** a single solid wall is fine. Don't fight clutter.
- **Cuts:** every 2–3 seconds. TikTok algorithm punishes static talking heads after the first 5 seconds.
- **Captions:** burn them into the video (not just the platform's auto-captions). Roughly 85% of TikTok and 60% of IG views happen muted.
- **B-roll:** screen recordings are your friend. Record yourself using `/roi-audit` and the Arora chat widget — that's 80% of your B-roll for free.
- **Batch-record:** 3 hours on a Saturday gives you 6-8 videos. Edit on Sunday. Schedule via Buffer or Later. Don't try to post daily live.
