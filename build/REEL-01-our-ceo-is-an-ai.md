## 🛠️ Production workflow — exact steps

### Step 1 · Generate the 10 shots in Higgsfield (60-90 min)
- Open Higgsfield, new project, **9:16 vertical**
- Paste prompt for shot 1 → generate → re-roll if not perfect, max 3 attempts → download
- Repeat for shots 2-10
- Save each as `shot-01.mp4`, `shot-02.mp4`, etc.

### Step 2 · Generate the voiceover (10 min)
- **ElevenLabs:** paste the full voiceover script, choose Adam or Brian, generate, download as `vo.mp3`
- If ElevenLabs unavailable: **OpenAI TTS** (alloy or onyx voice) via the playground — slightly less natural but free with API
- Listen back. Re-generate if pacing feels off.

### Step 3 · Source the music (10 min)
- Pick from the sources above, download as `music.mp3`
- Trim to 38 seconds

### Step 4 · Stitch in a video editor (45 min)
Free options: **CapCut** (desktop or mobile), **DaVinci Resolve** (free, more powerful)

Sequence:
1. Drop voiceover on track 1
2. Drop music on track 2 (under voiceover, -18dB)
3. Drop shots 1-10 on the video track, snapped to the timing in this brief
4. Add text overlays per the brief — Space Grotesk Bold (download free at Google Fonts)
5. Add subtle 0.3s crossfade between shots (DO NOT use longer transitions — they kill TikTok pacing)
6. Burn captions matching the voiceover (CapCut has auto-caption — clean up the formatting)
7. Add 0.5s fade-to-black at 0:37.5

### Step 5 · Export + post (15 min)
- Export as 1080×1920 H.264 MP4, 30fps, ~10Mbps bitrate
- Upload to TikTok with caption: `Our CEO is an AI. Here's what 6 months of AI-led operations actually looks like. 🟣 aiprosol.com`
- Hashtags: `#AI #AIAgents #Automation #StartupTok #BuildInPublic`
- Cross-post to Instagram Reels with the same caption (drop the `#StartupTok` hashtag — IG-native ones in the content pack)
- Pin to your profile

## ⚠️ Common Higgsfield pitfalls to avoid

- **Don't** ask Higgsfield to generate text inside the frame — it usually renders garbled letters. Always add text in the editor in step 4.
- **Don't** use prompts with "Aiprosol" or any brand name — Higgsfield doesn't know the brand and will guess. The shot prompts above describe the *concept* (violet orb, particle logo), not the literal brand name.
- **Don't** over-prompt. Higgsfield ignores most adjectives past ~80 words. The prompts above are deliberately tight.
- **Don't** mix aspect ratios across shots — set 9:16 once at project level and never change it.
- **Do** add `cinematic 9:16, 24fps, depth of field` to every prompt — it forces the consistent look.
