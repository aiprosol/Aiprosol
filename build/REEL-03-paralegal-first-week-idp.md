# AIPROSOL · REEL #3 · "POV: paralegal's first week with IDP"
## Higgsfield production brief — Hargreaves & Sterling empathy hit

Reel #1 sells the brand. Reel #2 sells the **contrast**. Reel #3 sells the **relief** — the moment a stressed-out human in a heavy-doc role realises their job didn't disappear, just got 45 hours back.

- **Length:** 45 seconds (10 shots, average 4.5s each — three shots run long)
- **Aspect:** Vertical 9:16, 1080×1920
- **Style:** Faceless POV. The camera *is* the paralegal — desk-level angle, never above eye-level, never showing hands or body. Hands-free POV is the hardest faceless format in Higgsfield, so the pitfalls section is doubled.
- **Voiceover:** Arora — empathetic in shots 1–4, confident from shot 5 onward
- **Music bed:** Monday-morning melancholy → tension build → release at shot 5 → uplift
- **Hook (first 3s):** *"POV: you're a paralegal. Monday morning. Forty-seven contracts on your desk."*
- **Primary CTA:** `aiprosol.com/services/intelligent-document-processing` (deeper than the ROI Audit — viewers of this reel are already qualified by legal context)
- **Fallback CTA:** `aiprosol.com/roi-audit` if you want one shared destination across the trilogy

## Common Higgsfield pitfalls + fallbacks (POV edition)

This reel has more pitfalls than the others because faceless-POV is the hardest format for any text-to-video model.

| Pitfall | Fallback |
|---|---|
| Higgsfield renders hands/arms in the POV shots despite negatives | **Strengthen negatives:** "no people, no hands, no arms, no fingers, no skin, no body parts visible." Reroll 3×. If still bad, accept a top-down "tabletop" framing instead of true first-person — frame the desk from directly above. |
| The "POV" shot ends up at face-height showing a desk in the distance | Re-prompt explicitly: "camera positioned exactly 30 centimetres above desk surface, looking down at the desk, no view of room beyond the desk." |
| Contract papers in shot 2 show readable but gibberish legal text | **Don't fight it.** From a viewer's perspective they're seeing "papers" — the brain doesn't read the text on 9:16 mobile. If a paper is *too* prominent and the gibberish is distracting, mask it in post with a clean violet-tinted folder graphic. |
| Shot 4 time-lapse produces a jump-cut instead of smooth transitions | Render shot 4 as **three** separate clips (morning → midday → dusk) and crossfade them in CapCut. |
| Dashboard text in shot 6 comes out garbled | **Render the dashboard blank** in Higgsfield — just the panel frame and the orb. Type the EXCEPTION QUEUE content as a CapCut text overlay using Space Grotesk 800. |
| Violet renders wrong | Hex codes in-prompt: "#8B5CF6 primary, #C084FC accent." Color-grade in post if needed. |
| Shots return 5s, you need 4s | Trim in post. Always render long, trim down. |
| Brand orb doesn't match Reels #1 and #2 | **Reuse Reel #1's orb shot** wherever the orb appears (shots 5 and 9). Series continuity > novel render. |
