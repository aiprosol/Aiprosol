# AIPROSOL · REEL #4 · "You don't need a $50,000 consultant"
## Higgsfield production brief — pricing pattern-interrupt

Reel #1 sells the brand. Reel #2 sells the result. Reel #3 sells the relief. Reel #4 sells **against the alternative** — the $50k automation consultancy retainer. The reel is shorter, tighter, and more aggressive than the trilogy because the hook is competitive, not narrative.

- **Length:** 30 seconds (10 shots, average 3s each — fastest pacing of the series)
- **Aspect:** Vertical 9:16, 1080×1920
- **Style:** Faceless, brand-themed (violet `#8B5CF6` + dark `#0A0613`)
- **Voiceover:** Arora — confident, slightly punchier than #1-3 (energy +10%)
- **Music bed:** Tense rising → smash hit at shot 5 → confident drive to close
- **Hook (first 3s):** *"Stop paying fifty-thousand-dollar retainers to automation consultants who deliver a Zapier flow and disappear."*
- **Primary CTA:** `aiprosol.com/pricing` (depth target — viewer wants to *compare prices*)
- **Secondary CTA:** `aiprosol.com/roi-audit`

## Production workflow · ~2 hours total

1. **Render shots in Higgsfield** (~60 min). The desaturated office shots (1-4) may need re-rolls to hit the right colour drain. Save to `/Users/user/Airprosol/build/reels/04/shot-01.mp4` through `shot-10.mp4`.
2. **Generate voiceover in ElevenLabs** (~10 min). Single take. Export 48kHz mono WAV → `vo.wav`.
3. **Pick / generate music** (~10 min). Time the smash hit to land at 0:12.
4. **Assemble in CapCut / Premiere / Resolve** (~30 min). Drop shots V1, VO A2, music A3, SFX A4. The colour-grade contrast (desaturated → violet) is what sells the punchline — don't dilute it in post. Burn captions. Export 1080×1920 H.264 ~10 Mbps.
5. **Post** (~10 min). TikTok first. Caption from `CONTENT-PACK-IG-TIKTOK.md` piece #5.

## Common Higgsfield pitfalls + fallbacks

| Pitfall | Fallback |
|---|---|
| Shots 1-4 come out too violet (not muted enough) | Add to prompt: "desaturated, almost monochromatic, cold green-grey office tones, no warm colours." Re-roll 2-3×. If still violet, fix in CapCut by adding a desaturation LUT (-40% saturation, +10% blue). |
| Shots 6-8 have card text rendered as gibberish | **Render the cards blank in Higgsfield** — just the violet-bordered frame + background. Type the actual numbers and labels as CapCut text overlays. |
| Shot 5 smash cut isn't sharp enough | Add to prompt: "hard cut with motion-blur trail, no transition, instant frame change." In post: trim 2-3 frames at the cut point to tighten it further. |
| Voiceover energy doesn't match the tonal split | Record two takes — restrained for 0-12s, energised for 12-30s — crossfade at the 12s mark. |
| Brand orb in shot 5 doesn't match Reels #1-3 | Reuse Reel #1's orb shot. Series continuity. |

## Why this reel is different

- **Shortest of the series.** 30s vs 35-45s for the trilogy. Pricing-comparison content rewards brevity.
- **Most aggressive tone.** "Stop overpaying" is unusually direct for Aiprosol's voice. Justified because the alternative ($50k consultants) is genuinely a bad deal for most SMBs, and timidity here reads as uncertainty.
- **Deeper CTA than the trilogy.** Sends to `/pricing` not `/roi-audit` — because viewers who watched 30s of pricing comparison are *already at the audit step in their head*. They want to compare prices.
- **Pillar A→B handoff.** Reel #4 closes Tier 1's launch cluster and opens the Pillar B (ROI Receipt) rotation. After this ships, the cadence becomes weekly receipt-style posts.
