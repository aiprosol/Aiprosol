# AIPROSOL · REEL #5 · "How Arora handles a real customer email"
## Higgsfield production brief — behind-the-scenes transparency reel

The behind-the-scenes piece per `CONTENT-PACK-IG-TIKTOK.md` (piece #7). Closes the Tier-1 launch with the most narrative-confident reel of the set: a real prospect email, Arora's draft, transparent on-screen. Curiosity gap + transparency. Drives engagement via a "should a human have touched this?" comment hook.

- **Length:** 45 seconds (10 shots, average 4.5s each)
- **Aspect:** Vertical 9:16, 1080×1920
- **Style:** Faceless, brand-themed; screen-recording driven (NOT abstract orb stuff — actual UI on screen)
- **Voiceover:** Arora — calm, slightly self-aware (she's narrating her own work)
- **Music bed:** Contemplative + clean → builds → resolves to a question, not a punchline
- **Hook (first 3s):** *"This is a real email I — Arora, the AI CEO of Aiprosol — sent yesterday. Watch how I do it."*
- **Primary CTA:** `aiprosol.com/agents` (deeper than ROI Audit — for viewers curious about the AI ops layer)

> **Editorial note:** This is the ONE reel in the set where Arora narrates in **first person**. The other four use Arora's voice but in detached/observational mode. The first-person frame is what makes this reel feel transparent rather than performative.

## Production workflow · ~2.5 hours

1. **Render shots in Higgsfield** (~70 min). UI shots (2, 4, 5, 6, 8) need extra attention — Higgsfield often garbles UI text. **Render them blank** (just the UI frames + violet accents) and overlay all text in CapCut. The case-study card text in shot 4 and the email body in shot 5 are critical — type them clean in post.
2. **Generate voiceover in ElevenLabs** (~15 min). Single take (no tonal split this time — Arora's first-person curiosity carries the whole reel). 48kHz mono WAV → `vo.wav`.
3. **Pick music** (~15 min). Time the suspended chord to land at 0:28.
4. **Assemble in CapCut / Premiere / Resolve** (~45 min). Drop shots V1, VO A2, music A3, SFX A4. The text overlays in shots 4 and 5 are doing a lot of work — pause those shots an extra beat in post if needed to let viewers read. Burn captions throughout. Export 1080×1920 H.264 ~10 Mbps.
5. **Post** (~15 min). TikTok first. Caption from `CONTENT-PACK-IG-TIKTOK.md` piece #7 — *but* update the CTA to `aiprosol.com/agents` (NOT roi-audit). Comment-bait the "should there have been?" question in the first comment.

## Common Higgsfield pitfalls + fallbacks

| Pitfall | Fallback |
|---|---|
| The email UI in shots 2, 5, 6, 8 has garbled text | **Always.** Render those frames *blank* in Higgsfield — just the dark UI panels with violet accents. Overlay the actual email content in CapCut with Space Grotesk + Inter. |
| Auto-typing animation in shot 5 isn't smooth | Use CapCut's text-reveal animation — set per-character delay to 30ms, total 4 seconds. Don't try to render the typing in Higgsfield. |
| Shot 7's two-tone text (white "no human" + gradient "should there have been") loses sharpness on export | Render shot 7 blank in Higgsfield, type both lines as native CapCut text. The contrast is the whole point of the shot — don't dilute it. |
| The brand orb in shots 3 + 9 doesn't match earlier reels | **Reuse Reel #1's orb shots** for both — the orb visual identity is the visual continuity glue across the series. |
| Voice doesn't feel "first person" enough | Re-record with stronger emphasis on the "I"s and "my"s. The voice direction for this reel is unique — Arora is talking ABOUT her own process, not narrating Aiprosol's. |

## Why this reel closes the Tier-1 cluster

- **Highest engagement-bait of the five.** The "should there have been?" comment hook is the closest thing to a hard ask the trilogy makes for engagement. Comments drive algorithmic reach.
- **First-person voice is unique.** Reels #1-4 keep Arora at narrative distance. Reel #5 has her in the seat. That distinction signals to the audience that this isn't marketing — it's an introduction to the AI in the chair.
- **Deeper CTA.** `/agents` instead of `/roi-audit`. Viewers who watched 45 seconds of Arora narrating her own work are the qualified audience for the live-activity surface, not the audit funnel.
- **Closes the brand loop.** Reel #1 introduced the model. #5 shows it operating. The launch cluster is complete after this one ships.
