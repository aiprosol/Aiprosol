## Canva template build steps · ~15 minutes one-time

1. **New design** → 1080×1350 → background `#0A0613`
2. **Add the radial glow** — circle, 800px, fill `#8B5CF6`, transparency 60%, blur 60px
3. **Add the eyebrow chip** as a group: rectangle + text, group together, save to Canva "Brand Kit" as a reusable component
4. **Type the `$95,000`** in Space Grotesk 800 at 240pt, apply gradient fill via Canva Pro (or render as image and import if free tier)
5. **Add all the other text layers** per the spec
6. **Add the stack callout card**
7. **Add footer (logo + URL)**
8. **Save as "Aiprosol ROI Receipt Template"** in Canva
9. **For each subsequent post:** duplicate, swap the number, eyebrow industry, customer name, context, stack callout. ~5 minutes per post after that.

## Why this format works

- **One big number stops the scroll.** The `$95,000` reads from across a room. Mobile users scrolling will see it before they see anything else.
- **Industry tag in the eyebrow** lets the right ICP recognize themselves in 0.5 seconds.
- **DM hook in the caption** adds engagement signal to the algorithm. Every `retail` DM is a warmer lead than a comment.
- **Repeatable.** Six pre-designed variants gives you ~2 months of Pillar B content without re-thinking the format.
- **Works on LinkedIn too** — same dimensions, same image. Cross-post for ~2× the reach for ~0× the extra work.

## Posting & A/B notes

- **First post (this one):** Friday Week 1, single-platform — Instagram first. Watch engagement for 24 hours. If it pulls reach above 1.5× your baseline single-post, cross-post to LinkedIn on Monday.
- **A/B test:** Same number, two variants — one with the stack callout card, one without (just the number + customer + footer). The cleaner variant sometimes pulls more saves because there's less to read. Keep both designs in Canva.
- **Long-tail use:** This template becomes the visual frame for *every* customer testimonial you ever publish. Even after Tier 1 cadence finishes, you'll keep using this format.
