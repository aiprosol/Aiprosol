1. Open `chatgpt-business-prompts.json` (or .csv for spreadsheet view).
2. Filter by category + subcategory to find what you need.
3. Copy the `system` + `user_template` into ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM.
4. Replace `{{tokens}}` with your specifics.
5. Read the `notes` for when-to-use guidance + watch-outs.
