Part 1 · The Readiness Scorecard (120 questions × 12 dimensions)

The scorecard auto-aggregates into a single 0-100 readiness score plus a per-dimension breakdown that drives prioritisation.

Dimension 1 — Data foundation (10 questions)

Examples: "Do we have a unified customer record across systems?" · "Are the top-3 datasets queryable without engineering?" · "Is data lineage documented for revenue, customer, product?"

Dimension 2 — Identity + access (10 questions)

"Do we have SSO across all production systems?" · "Are role-based access controls audited quarterly?" · "Do we have an offboarding checklist that completes in <24h?"

Dimension 3 — Tool architecture (10 questions)

"How many SaaS tools do we operate?" · "Are integrations point-to-point or hub-and-spoke?" · "Is there a workflow orchestrator already in place?"

Dimension 4 — AI literacy (10 questions)

"What % of staff have used a frontier LLM in the last 30 days?" · "Do we have an internal Slack channel for AI use cases?" · "Is there a designated AI owner?"

Dimension 5 — Governance + risk (10 questions)

"Do we have an AI usage policy?" · "Is there a process for approving new AI tools?" · "Are AI-generated outputs labelled as such?"

Dimension 6 — Compliance posture (10 questions)

"GDPR, CCPA, SOC2 status?" · "Data residency requirements?" · "Customer DPA terms when using AI processors?"

Dimension 7 — Talent (10 questions)

"Do we have someone who can specify an AI workflow?" · "Build it?" · "Operate it day-to-day?" · "Audit it for drift?"

Dimension 8 — Process maturity (10 questions)

"Are our top-5 business processes documented?" · "Are SLAs measured?" · "Is there a recurring process improvement cadence?"

Dimension 9 — Change management (10 questions)

"How does the org typically respond to new tools?" · "Who owns adoption?" · "Is there a feedback loop after rollouts?"

Dimension 10 — Financial model (10 questions)

"Can we measure ROI of automation projects?" · "Is there a budget line for AI tooling?" · "Are we tracking task-cost vs. fully-loaded-employee-cost?"

Dimension 11 — Customer impact (10 questions)

"Are customer-facing AI features labelled?" · "Is there a customer feedback channel for AI outputs?" · "Are we A/B testing AI vs. human?"

Dimension 12 — Strategic clarity (10 questions)

"Do we have a 12-month AI roadmap?" · "Is the board briefed quarterly?" · "Is there a defensible thesis for why AI is core to our business?"

Part 2 · Vendor Selection RFP Template

A drop-in Word document for procurement. Cover sheet, 40-criteria evaluation grid, and 10 red-flag screening questions.

40 evaluation criteria across 8 categories

  1. Technical fit (8 criteria): Integration footprint, API quality, latency SLAs, model choice, fine-tuning support, on-prem option, audit logs, data residency
  2. Commercial (5): Pricing model, lock-in clauses, termination, SLAs/credits, professional services included
  3. Security (5): SOC2 Type 2, ISO 27001, GDPR DPA, encryption-at-rest + in-transit, vulnerability disclosure policy
  4. Support (5): Tiered support availability, response SLAs, named account manager, dedicated Slack/email channel, status page
  5. Roadmap alignment (4): Public roadmap, feature request process, customer advisory board, version cadence
  6. Reference customers (4): Customers in our industry, customers our size, case studies with measurable outcomes, references willing to speak
  7. Implementation support (5): Onboarding sprint, dedicated CSM, training included, sandbox environment, migration assistance
  8. Long-term viability (4): Funding runway, parent company status, customer count trajectory, employee glassdoor signal

10 red-flag screening questions

Each vendor must answer in writing. Any "No" or evasive answer is grounds to exclude from shortlist:

  1. Can you provide a SOC 2 Type 2 report dated within the last 12 months?
  2. Will you sign our standard DPA without modification?
  3. Do you commit to a written data deletion within 30 days of contract termination?
  4. Can you provide three reference customers in our industry?
  5. What is your customer churn rate over the last 12 months?
  6. Have you been acquired or are you actively raising capital?
  7. Will you indemnify us against IP infringement claims from your training data?
  8. Do you guarantee a written notice of 30+ days before any breaking API change?
  9. Can we run a 30-day technical evaluation in a sandbox before signing?
  10. Will you commit to a maximum 30-day onboarding window with named milestones?
